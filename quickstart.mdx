## Get Started in Minutes

Follow these steps to create your EasyShoppingClub account, set up a documentation space, and upload your first documents. You complete the entire process in under five minutes. EasyShoppingClub provides a simple web interface and CLI for flexible workflows.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Bookmark your dashboard after signup. Use the `{API_KEY}` from account settings for CLI authentication.
</Callout>

## Step-by-Step Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.easyshoppingclub.com/signup` and enter your email, name, and password. Verify your email via the confirmation link.

    Choose your plan during signup:
    - Free: Up to 5 spaces, 100 documents.
    - Pro: Unlimited spaces, advanced search.

    After verification, you land on the dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up a Documentation Space" icon="folder-plus">
    Click **New Space** on the dashboard.

    Enter details:
    - Name: `My Project Docs`
    - Description: `Documentation for my e-commerce app`
    - Brand Color: `#3B82F6`

    Click **Create**. Your space appears in the sidebar with a default `index.mdx`.

    <Image
      src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1460925895917-afdab827c52f?w=800&fit=crop"
      alt="New documentation space dashboard"
      width="800"
      height="400"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Your First Documents" icon="upload-cloud">
    Add content via web upload or CLI.

    Copy your `{SPACE_ID}` and `{API_KEY}` from space settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload Methods

Choose web upload for quick files or CLI for automation.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web UI" icon="globe">
    Drag files into the space editor or click **Upload**.

    Supported formats: `.mdx`, `.md`, `.txt`, images, PDFs.

    Reorder documents in the sidebar after upload.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="CLI" icon="terminal">
    Install the CLI first.

    <CodeGroup tabs="npm,yarn">
```bash
npm install -g @easyshoppingclub/cli
```
```bash
yarn global add @easyshoppingclub/cli
```
    </CodeGroup>

    Authenticate and upload:

````bash
esc login --api-key YOUR_API_KEY
esc upload ./docs/intro.mdx --space my-project-docs
````
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
